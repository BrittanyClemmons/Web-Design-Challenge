Wind Speed


Wind speeds are noticeably calm at 0 to 5 mph across most observations shown in the 80 to -20 degree lines of latitude with peaks in wind speed at the 60 degree and -40 degree lines of latitude. Top wind speeds are observed at 35 mph between 40 and 80 degree lines of latitude. A significant drop in wind speed is also observed at 15 mph and less between the -20 to 0 degree lines of latitude. Natural events such as the global polar and tropical jet streams could have had an impact on wind speed observations at the time this data was collected.
